Structural Notes

This blend co-formulates two distinct synthetic peptides commonly co-administered in research protocols on tissue repair. BPC-157 is a 15-amino-acid pentadecapeptide derived from a fragment of body protection compound. TB-500 is the commercial designation for synthetic full-length thymosin beta-4, a 43-amino-acid G-actin sequestering peptide. The two peptides are structurally unrelated; the blend is a mass-based co-lyophilization, not a covalent conjugate.
